California


California has many destinations, including cities, national parks, and beaches.

  1. Los Angeles: The second-largest city in the US, and a global center for trade and finance.
  2. San Diego: A coastal city in California known for its beaches and naval base.
  3. Santa Monica: A city in California famous for its iconic pier and beach.
  4. Santa Barbara: A picturesque city in California with Spanish-style architecture.
  5. Yosemite National Park: A national park in California known for its waterfalls and giant sequoia trees.
  • Cioppino: An Italian fish stew that originated in San Francisco's Fisherman's Wharf in the late 1800s. Some say it's the truest reflection of San Francisco's history and location.
  • Crab Louie: An iconic California dish from San Francisco.
  • Fortune Cookies: An iconic California dish from San Francisco.
  • Chicken Tetrazzini: An iconic California dish from San Francisco.
  • Almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and pecans: California is known for its almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and pecans.
